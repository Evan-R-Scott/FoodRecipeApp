About



Welcome to Dish Discovery, your ultimate destination for personalized food recipes. We understand that finding the perfect recipe can be a challenging task, especially when you're looking for something specific. Hopefully we can make that hurdle easier and your culinary journey more enjoyable.


  • Food: We believe that your cravings should always take center stage. In the "Recipe" box, you can input the ingredients you'd like to include in your recipe.
  • Exclude Ingredients: We understand that dietary restrictions, allergies, or simply personal preferences may lead you to exclude certain ingredients from your meals. In the "Exclude Ingredients" box, you can specify what you'd like to avoid, ensuring that the recipes we suggest are tailored to your needs.
  • Diet: Maintaining a specific diet, whether it's vegetarian, vegan, keto, paleo, or any other, is a commitment that deserves respect. With our "Diet" option, you can filter recipes that align with your dietary goals.
